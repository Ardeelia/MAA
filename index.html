Marathalli Alumni Association

Welcome to the official website of the Marathalli Alumni Association. We've started the association in order to grow and support our amazing community.



What does MAA do?

We started MAA to support fellow alumni as well as students at Allen Marathalli in their journey. We aim to provide help and advice to everyone involved in this community.

The MAA has been started very recently and we'd appreciate any constructive advice as well as people looking to reach out and help in furthering the association. Please reach out to the people listed in the contacts section and they'll direct you to us.

We wanted to provide insights into how our life was like both at Allen Marathalli as well as after our tenure there. We also thought, as a help to the students, we'd provide some resources which we found helpful and hope could help others.

We hope that this website can serve as a place for us to interact with each other and have fun learning!
